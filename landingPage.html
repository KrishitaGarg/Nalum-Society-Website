A warm welcome to the alumni, students, and friends of NSUT!

NSUT Alumni Society, Nalum, is a global network of alumni and students of Netaji Subhas University of Technology (NSUT). Our mission is to foster lifelong relationships with all to support the university’s growth and development. We provide opportunities for students and alumni to connect with each other, engage with the university, and contribute to the NSUT community.

Whether your goal is to reconnect, expand your professional network, support NSUT’s growth, or explore the impact of giving back, you’ve come to the right place. This portal is your gateway to engaging with the vibrant NSUT community, participating in alumni activities, and discovering ways to make a meaningful contribution. If you’re passionate about giving back, we’ll guide you on how to create a lasting impact, direct your support to where it matters most, and leave a meaningful legacy. Looking to reconnect? Dive into our extensive alumni network, career resources, and upcoming events. Rediscover the NSUT that inspires and drives you. Alumni, students, and friends like you are the driving force behind NSUT’s journey to new heights. Join us today and celebrate the pride of being part of the NSUT family!
